CHAPTER 1: PERSONAL FINANCE BASICS

Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this chapter, students would be able to:

1.1 Describe the concept and importance of personal financial planning and recognize some basic considerations in an individual's financial planning decisions. (LO1, C1)
1.2 Identify the step-to-step personal financial process. (LO2, C2)
1.3 Apply the SMART framework in setting and prioritizing personal financial goals and objectives. (LO3, C3)
This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of personal financial planning, exploring its importance in achieving financial goals and managing resources effectively. You will learn about the systematic process of financial planning and how to set SMART financial goals.
